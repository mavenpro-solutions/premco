About Us

Engineering India’s rail and industrial future with over three decades of excellence.

Explore Our Expertise

Building India's Infrastructure Backbone Since 1974

Premco Rail Engineers Limited is a trusted name in rail infrastructure and industrial construction across India. With over three decades of proven expertise, we have built a strong legacy of delivering end-to-end turnkey solutions for both Indian Railways and private sector industries.

Our capabilities span the full spectrum of infrastructure development — from civil engineering, electrification, and signaling to telecommunication and core industrial construction. Every project we undertake is guided by precision, innovation, and a commitment to quality.

At Premco, we go beyond conventional construction.

We don’t just build — we revive possibilities. As pioneers in stalled project recovery, we specialize in diagnosing challenges, reengineering solutions, and steering complex projects back to success.

Driven by experience, powered by expertise, and grounded in integrity, Premco Rail Engineers Limited continues to build the infrastructure that moves India forward.
